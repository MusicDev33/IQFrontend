Getting Started

Getting started with Inquantir is easy!

After you've created a profile, just click on the "Ask Inquantir" button in your navbar:

When asking a question, you'll be prompted to add a subject and a source. The source is where the question came from, whether that's your textbook, or your homework software. Sometimes, the source might not have been added to Inquantir yet. In this case, you can add it yourself, but make sure to include edition numbers in the textbook title.

After you've added a subject and source to your question, you now just have to wait for it to be answered!
