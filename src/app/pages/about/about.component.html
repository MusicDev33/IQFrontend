Inquantir is...

A Learning Platform

Inquantir aims to help learning by giving answers that explain the topic, as opposed to just giving the solution. Help on your homework doesn't need to be spread across multiple sites.

Free

Our platform is totally free! As a user, you can ask and answer questions for free, as well as read and write other answers.

High-Quality

Inquantir is devoted to high-quality answers, and relies on our users and verified faculty to do so. Users can vote on answers and questions, allowing Inquantir to decide which ones to keep.

The mission of Inquantir is to provide academic help without charging more money on top of tuition and textbook costs. We emphasize understanding of the material, as opposed to just giving out answers. It doesn't matter whether or not you're a student, everyone is welcome!
